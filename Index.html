72-hour job hunt playbook
Apply to 80–100 jobs with ATS-tailored resumes + direct recruiter outreach. Volume × targeting = interviews.
1
Run ATS Analyzer on your resume — do this first
Go to the ATS tab. Paste any JD + your resume. Fix anything below 75%. Build 3 resume variants: one for Data Analyst/BA, one for Data Engineer/Python Dev, one for ML/AI/GenAI. Save each as PDF.
2
Morning block 9am–12pm: LinkedIn + Naukri
Filter: Last 24 hours · Fresher / 0–1 yr. Use LinkedIn Easy Apply first (fastest pipeline). Target 20 applications. Naukri has the highest volume for Indian freshers — use the Freshers filter.
3
Evening block 7pm–10pm: Wellfound + CutShort + Indeed
Wellfound is best for startup roles and very fresher-friendly. CutShort for Python/ML. Indeed with "Posted Today" filter. Another 20 applications. 40+ applications per day is the minimum target.
4
10 LinkedIn outreach messages daily — non-negotiable
After each application, find the recruiter or HR on LinkedIn. Use the Outreach tab to generate a personalized message. Send it. Companies that receive direct outreach hire 2× faster than those that don't.
5
Follow up at 48h, track everything in the Tracker tab
Data in the Tracker saves between sessions (stored in your browser). After 48h silence, send the follow-up message. Move fast — jobs posted within 24h get filled within 72–96h.
